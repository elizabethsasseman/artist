Designed a Desktop UI Flow

This project displays a flow of mockups leading a user to plan a leave of absence from work for a family health condition. Shanti Gupta is our user in this flow and she is planning a leave of absence for her daughter, Saavni Gupta. Shanti is planning her leave through an absence management portal set up for her through work.

Login Page

Shanti Gupta needs to plan a leave of absence from work to care for her sick daughter, Saavni. Shanti arrives at the login page of the portal and has a very pleasant initial impression. It's currently springtime and the vibrant image she sees of tiny plants gives Shanti a brief moment of joy in thinking about her own vibrant garden. Shanti can use all the joy she can get these days with everything going on. Shanti notices a huge difference in how welcoming the site is now compared to how it was when she last logged in. Shanti goes ahead and logs in.

Below is how this screen used to look.

Landing Page

Upon arriving on the main landing page, Shanti immediately notices a leave that is currently open for herself. The gray checkmarks remind her she still needs to complete one of her tasks, although she is not here to do this right now. Shanti scans the page and quickly notices a blue button that says, "Plan a Leave". She clicks it assuming without a doubt that this will take her to the location where she will be able to plan a leave for Saavni. The last time Shanti logged in here it took her what felt like forever to figure out how to plan a leave.

Splash Page

Shanti is taken to a page with a few friendly reminders. This helps her confirm that she has everything she needs in order to successfully plan her leave. Shanti determines she has all the correct items and clicks, "Begin".

Plan a Leave

The following mockups show Shanti's flow through each page in filling out information related to her leave for her daughter, Saavni. After Shanti reviews her information on the Review & Submit page she clicks, "Submit".

Splash Page

Shanti is given a message confirmation the successful submission of her leave request. She feels good knowing she simply needs to wait for her acknowledgement letter to arrive. Shanti is also pleased with the imagery once again. The visual gives her a very warm and comforting feeling. Shanti clicks "OK" and is taken back to the main landing page.

Landing Page

Upon arriving back at the main landing page, Shanti notices the addtion of an open leave card to the right of her other open leave. She can clearly tell from this that she needs to complete her paperwork as soon as it arrives in the mail. Shanti is curious what additional information she'd be able to see if she clicked on the "View Detais" button. She goes ahead and clicks it.

Details Page

Shanti is taken to a page with variout details related to her leave. There isn't anything she needs to currently take a closer look at here so she feels it's time to log out. Shanti finds the "Log Out" link in the corner and clicks it.

Logout Page

Upon logging out, Shanti once again sees the vibrant image she first saw upon logging in and is inspired to get outside for some fresh air.
