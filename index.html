UX Designer

Hello! I am a UX designer, visual designer, product developer, photographer, and entrepreneur with experience taking creative ideas from design to development. I focus on creating and capturing simplicity through user-centric design.

I have experience collaborating with a variety of individuals and teams, including other UX designers, software engineers, business owners, manufacturers, marketing teams, and product managers. I've worked for large SaaS companies on an Agile/Lean team as well as for small start-ups.

I am incredibly motivated by new ideas, brainstorming sessions, and other passionate people who love to think outside the box. Recently, I've been working as a freelance designer. I spent a few months introducing and implementing UX design into a small start-up, I started my own business and took my first physical product from manufacturing to market, and I've been working through a unique idea of pairing photography with interiors. My ability to see big picture vison and think through big ideas is my greatest asset.

My goal is to help build successful companies who's missions and values I believe in through the creation of usable products that better the world. I have interests in UX design thinking, interaction design, industrial design, architecture, interior design, regenerative agriculture, and landscape design.

I am at my best when thinking through new ideas, reading non-fiction, running and climbing in the mountains, working in the garden, and road-tripping across the country.
