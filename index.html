About
My name's Elizabeth and I'm a model and photographer based out of Boulder, Colorado. As an artist, I strive to capture what feels good and what inspires. Not only do I find passion in modeling and photography but also in user interface design, product development, writing and painting.

I live for gorgeous scenery, good music, summiting mountains and rock climbs, inspiring books, delicious food, running in the mountains and road tripping. Anything that excites my five senses also ignites my inner desire to create. I use my creativity to explore every lingering feeling and thought with the hopes of creating something new and beautiful out of it.
