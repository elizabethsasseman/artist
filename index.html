Designed a New Spotify Feature

I realized something needed to change when I shuffled one of my Spotify playlists and found that a bunch of songs that are split into parts, played out of order. I decided to design a feature to present to Spotify that would prevent these parts from shuffling when added to a shuffled playlist.

Developed an Idea for an Estate-Planning App

After watching my family struggle to find important paperwork related to a loved one's estate, it became apparent that a simple process did not exist in the realm of estate-planning or in settling someone's estate. Initially I wanted to build a product designed to streamline the process of working with an estate-planning attorney. The vision of my product evolved to include a platform where value between clients and attorneys would be exchanged.
